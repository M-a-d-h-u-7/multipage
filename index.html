Philosophy

More Than a Watch.
A Legacy.

AURUM was born from a simple belief: that a timepiece should outlast its owner — passed from hand to hand, generation to generation, carrying with it the weight of moments lived. We do not make watches for collectors alone. We make them for people who understand that how you spend your time defines who you are.

Our History

A Century of
Horological Excellence

1892

The Founding

Édouard Aurum, a young watchmaker from Le Locle, Switzerland, establishes his atelier with a single ambition: to create watches of uncompromising quality that would endure for centuries. His first piece, the Calibre 1 pocket watch, takes six months to complete.

1924

The First Wristwatch

AURUM introduces its first wristwatch, the Elégante, with an in-house movement featuring a barrel spring of unprecedented tension. The Elégante is worn by royalty across three continents.

1951

The Chronograph Breakthrough

Master watchmaker Henri Aurum develops the AU-Chrono movement — a column-wheel chronograph mechanism with a lateral coupling that remains 35% more accurate than any contemporary design. It wins the Geneva Prize for Technical Innovation.

1978

The Tourbillon Collection

AURUM presents its first tourbillon — a gravity-defying escapement housed in a hand-engraved rose gold case. Only 12 are made. All 12 sell within days of the announcement.

2003

Global Expansion

The third generation of the Aurum family opens boutiques in Paris, New York, Hong Kong, and Dubai. AURUM becomes a truly global name while maintaining the intimacy of a family manufacture.

2025

The New Collection

AURUM launches its most ambitious collection to date — 85 distinct references across six series, each featuring an updated in-house movement with silicon escapement and 96-hour power reserve.

Our Principles

What We Stand For

⚙️

Mechanical Mastery

Every movement is designed, developed, and assembled in-house at our Geneva manufacture. We employ 85 full-time watchmakers, each specialised in a single discipline — from regulating to engraving.

💎

Material Integrity

We source only the finest materials on earth. Sapphire crystals grown in our own facility. Cases machined from aerospace-grade steel. Dials decorated with genuine guilloché and grand feu enamel.

🕰️

Enduring Design

We do not follow trends. An AURUM design must be equally beautiful in 1950 and 2050. Every proportion, every marker, every hand is refined over hundreds of prototype iterations before approval.

⚙ ⚙

The Atelier

Where Every
Second is Born

Our manufacture in Geneva spans 6,000 square metres across five floors. Within it, 380 distinct operations are performed to complete a single watch — from raw metal to finished timepiece.

Visitors to our atelier often remark on the silence. In an age of machines, our craftspeople work with hands guided by decades of muscle memory.

Movement Architecture

Each calibre is designed by our in-house engineers using proprietary CAD software developed over 20 years.

Component Fabrication

Over 280 individual parts are machined in-house to tolerances of ±2 microns.

Hand Assembly & Regulation

Each movement is assembled by a single master watchmaker and regulated for 20 days across six positions.

Final Quality Control

216 individual quality checks. Water resistance tested to 150% of stated specification.
