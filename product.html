The Chronograph Noir is a testament to AURUM's mastery over the most complex complications in horology. Born in our Geneva atelier and requiring 380 individual parts, each assembled by hand, the Noir embodies a decade of research into the perfect chronograph.

The black lacquer dial is applied in seven successive coats, each hand-polished before the next is laid down. The applied gold indices are set at perfect angles to maximise legibility. The column-wheel chronograph mechanism can be seen through the exhibition caseback — a miniature theatre of precision engineering.

Each Chronograph Noir comes with two straps and a presentation box crafted from natural walnut.
